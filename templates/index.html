About

Global maps of the earthquakes that occured during the last week or last month. Earthquake data is gathered from earthquake.usgs.gov. Fault line data is collected from github.com/fraxen/tectonicplates. Magnitude is represented by darker color and greater marker size. Different map tiles are available from satellite view to street view.
